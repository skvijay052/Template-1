A Breeze of Pleasant Fragrance

Goloka presents a breeze carrying a pleasant fragrance from a garden of flowers and other natural ingredients which at once captures the organ of smell, due to which one's consciousness is saturated with devotion. It gives you a transcendental experience.

Goloka is well know for its products. It manufactures & exports Indian incense sticks, traditional incense sticks, florasense and Padmini incense sticks. Successfully capturing the mesmerizing range of fragrances, we have been preparing a wide range of incense sticks which include Indian Incense Sticks, Traditional Incense Sticks, Florasense Incense Sticks, Padmini Incense Stick, Natural Floral Incense Sticks, Natural Malayaja (Sandal) Incense Sticks, Perfumed Floral Incense Sticks, Natural Madhu Masala Incense Sticks, Dhoop Sticks, Puja Items and Good Earth Incense Sticks.
